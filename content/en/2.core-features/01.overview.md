## Moderation System
Fully structured moderation with case tracking, mass actions, and detailed escalation analytics. Warn, mute, timeout, kick, and ban users. Review case histories, track patterns, and generate reports without breaking a sweat.

**Key Features:**
- Warn, mute, timeout, kick, ban with case IDs
- Mass moderation for bulk operations
- Case history with escalation tracking
- Warning expiration system
- Moderator leaderboards and action analytics

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/moderation)

---

## Server Backup & Restore
Snapshot your entire server in compressed backups. Restore roles, channels, permissions, threads, emojis, bans, member roles, nicknames, and pinned messages. Up to 5 backups per guild with 10-minute cooldowns.

**Key Features:**
- Compressed full-server backups
- Restore roles, channels, categories, permissions
- Webhook, thread, emoji, and ban restoration
- Role mapping and selective restores
- Cooldown enforcement (5 per guild, 10min between)

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/backups)

---

## Advanced Logging & Audit Trails
Capture every meaningful server event. Messages, roles, channels, members, voice, webhooks, threads, emojis, invites, and configuration changes. Fully customizable per category and event—zero spam, pure accountability.

**Key Features:**
- Message edits, deletes, bulk deletes
- Role, channel, category, permission changes
- Member joins, leaves, updates, voice activity
- Webhook, thread, emoji, invite logging
- Per-event configuration and audit correlation

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/logging)

---

## Advanced Role Management
Persistent roles, timed roles, auto-roles, and bulk assignment. Roles survive member leaves and rejoin automatically. Timed roles expire themselves. Join-based roles assign on entry. All with safety checks.

**Key Features:**
- Persistent roles surviving member leave/rejoin
- Temporary roles with automatic expiration
- Auto-roles on member join
- Date-based join range targeting
- Bulk role assignment and removal with confirmation

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/roles)

---

## Advanced Message Purge
Surgical message cleanup. Purge by user, content type, patterns, links, embeds, reactions, roles, IDs, and date ranges. Real-time progress tracking with confirmation prompts and safety validations.

**Key Features:**
- Purge by user, bot, or content type
- Text pattern, link, embed, reaction filtering
- Role-based filtering and date range selection
- Real-time progress tracking
- Confirmation prompts and comprehensive safety checks

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/purge)

## Custom Boards with Smart Filtering
Unlimited reaction-based boards with custom thresholds and multiple emojis per board. Role, user, and channel filters. Self-star rules and automatic cleanup when reactions drop below threshold.

**Key Features:**
- Unlimited boards with custom reaction thresholds
- Multiple emoji support per board
- Self-star prevention and precise filtering
- Role, user, and channel filters
- Automatic updates and self-cleanup

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/boards)

---

## Live Counter Channels
Real-time server stats in auto-updating channels. Members, bots, roles, online count, boosts, and custom metrics. Place counters in voice, text, stage, or category channels for instant visibility.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time member and bot tracking
- Role population counters
- Online/offline user counts
- Server boost level tracking
- Voice, text, stage, and category placement

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/counters)

## AFK Status Management
Members set custom AFK status with reasons. Auto-notifies anyone who mentions them. Moderators view all statuses or manage individually. Auto-removes on return—because busy people shouldn't get harassed.

**Key Features:**
- Custom AFK status with personal reasons
- Automatic mention notifications
- Mention history tracking
- Moderator status management
- Auto-removal on member return

[Learn More →](/en/core-features/afk)

## Quick Feature Comparison

| Feature | Purpose | Best For |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Moderation** | Case-tracked moderation with analytics | Servers needing comprehensive mod tools |
| **Backups** | Full-server snapshots and restoration | Disaster recovery and safety nets |
| **Logging** | Complete audit trail of all events | Transparency and accountability |
| **Roles** | Persistent, timed, and auto-role assignment | Large servers and verification systems |
| **Purge** | Surgical message cleanup | Channel maintenance and spam control |
| **Boards** | Reaction-based content curation | Showcasing best content and highlights |
| **Counters** | Live server statistics | Growth tracking and milestones |
| **AFK** | Member availability tracking | Active communities with mentions |
| **Fun** | Community engagement and entertainment | Social servers and community building |
