# IANA Time Zones

::callout{icon="i-lucide-alert-circle" color="error"}
**Found an error or missing a timezone?**  
We’ve documented 595 timezone identifiers, but mistakes can happen. If you notice any errors or have suggestions, please help us improve! Contribute via our [GitHub](https://github.com/Lumiere-Docs) by tapping **Edit this page** or report an issue directly.
::

::callout{icon="i-lucide-info" color="info"}
To quickly find your timezone, use **Ctrl+F** (Windows), **Cmd+F** (Mac), or your device’s search feature on Android/iOS. You can also scroll the alphabetically arranged list to locate your country or region.
::
